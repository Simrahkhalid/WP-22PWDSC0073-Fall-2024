Explore Our Workout Routines

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced athlete, find workout routines that match your fitness level and help you achieve your goals. Explore, get inspired, and stay fit!

Featured Workout of the Week

Full-Body Strength Training

Strengthen your entire body with this powerful routine that combines strength, endurance, and flexibility. Ideal for intermediate to advanced levels.

  • Squats - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Deadlifts - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank - 3 sets of 45 seconds

Workout Categories

Cardio Workouts

Boost your endurance with cardio routines designed to improve heart health and stamina.

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) - 20 mins
  • Running - 5 km
  • Jump Rope - 15 mins

Strength Workouts

Build muscle and improve strength with routines focused on resistance and weight training.

  • Bench Press - 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Leg Press - 4 sets of 12 reps

Flexibility Workouts

Increase flexibility and range of motion with stretches and yoga-inspired movements.

  • Hamstring Stretch - 2 mins each leg
  • Downward Dog - 3 sets of 1 min
  • Spinal Twist - 2 mins each side
